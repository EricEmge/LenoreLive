About Lenore Live

🎸

Lenore Live emerged from the vibrant music scene with a unique sound that blends electronic elements with rock sensibilities. Formed in 2022, we've been dedicated to creating music that resonates with the soul and moves the body.

Our journey began in a small garage, fueled by passion and late-night jam sessions. Today, we're proud to share our music with fans around the world, always staying true to our roots while pushing creative boundaries.

With influences ranging from classic rock to modern electronic music, Lenore Live creates an atmosphere that's both nostalgic and forward-thinking. Every performance is an experience, every song tells a story.
